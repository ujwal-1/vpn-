Benefits of VPN
Provides secure and encrypted communication over public networks.
Protects user privacy and hides IP addresses.
Allows remote access to organizational networks.
Helps bypass geo-restrictions and censorship on websites or content.

Limitations of VPN
May reduce Internet speed due to encryption and rerouting.
Setup and maintenance can be costly or complex.
Some VPNs may have security or trust issues.
Certain websites or countries may block VPN usage.
